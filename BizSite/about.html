Hi There!

I'm Mike, a dedicated stay-at-home dad, navigating the chaos of parenting a 4-year-old boy, a 9-month-old daughter, and anticipating the arrival of another bundle of joy. My journey took an unexpected turn during the challenges of COVID, as I lost my job as a store manager at Stance Inc. It was a pivotal moment, with bills to pay and a 7-month-old son relying on me. Drawing from my marketing experience at Stance, I ventured into entrepreneurship, learning the ropes as I built a business from the ground up.

Driven by a desire to be present for my growing family, I hustled to build a large clientele and eventually transitioned us to a new home, prioritizing a work-from-home lifestyle. Currently, I wear the hat of a sales and marketing professional at a software company, overseeing a million dollars a year in ad spend across platforms like Google Ads, YouTube, and social media marketing.

My journey instilled in me a passion for helping small businesses, stemming from the challenges I faced when starting my own venture. Struggling to be discovered online due to budget constraints, I taught myself website development and design to overcome the hurdle. Now, armed with experience and time, I am on a mission to offer small businesses in Philly the chance I wish I had – a professionally crafted website without the burden of enormous upfront fees.
